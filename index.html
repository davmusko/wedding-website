Meet the Couple

-David & Jelena-

David and Jelena met through Catholic Match and had their first date on June 1st. From then on, Jelena knew David was someone special and wanted to get to know him more. David is from Troy, MI and his time spent traveling back and forth to Jelena solidified that the feeling was mutual. As they went on more dates, spent time together, and the BIG day of meeting the girls (Isabelle and Lucienne) had passed, Jelena knew he was going to stay around, not for just a few months, but for forever. He is a man of faith, hope, love and true kindness.

David has shown a deep love to the girls and continues to lead us all towards our Lord, bringing great peace. Jelena knows that her late husband had a guiding hand in sending his three girls someone so very special, and that someone is David. We can't wait to continue down this new adventure and see what the Lord has in store for our new family. We want to thank you all for the love, prayers, and support you have given us. We can't wait to celebrate with you all as we begin this new life together
